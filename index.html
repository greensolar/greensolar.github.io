What's New


#Commencement# I received my bachelor degree in Alternative Energy Science and Engineering with the honour of Outstanding Graduate in June, 2019. Five years in Shanghai, key words: horizon, peer-pressure, calling, and of course, full of memory! In September, I started the master journey specialized in Complex System Engineering and Management--Energy & Industry Track. #No regret in TU Delft#

In July 2018, the paper "An in-depth analysis of the silicon solar cell key parameters’ optimal magnitudes using PC1D simulations" was published by Optik-International Journal for Light and Electron Optics. In this study, the optimal magnitudes of silicon solar cell key parameters were simulated using the PC1D program and the carrier transmission mechanism behind that was analyzed.

On Feb. 25 2019, I finished the six-mmonth BD/Analysis internship at ENGIE, a global energy and services group. I actively tracked market intelligence and changes in the policy and competitive environment relevant to solid biomass sector, and conducted quantitative and qualitative reports analyzing the market potentials and risks. Besides, I participated in the evaluation of the target M&A firms.

From Mar. to Jun. 2018, I accomplished an individual project about district heating (DH) proposed by Sweco Denmark. The aim was to quantify the advantages and disadvantages of lower DH temperatures and perform a feasibility evaluation of the trainsition into (Ultra) low temperature district heating, covering financial, environmental, energy and socio-economic aspects.
